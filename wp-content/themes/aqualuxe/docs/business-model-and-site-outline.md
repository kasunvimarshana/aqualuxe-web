# AquaLuxe – Business Model and Website Content Outline

Date: 2025-09-05
Owner: Product & Marketing
Audience: Founders, Marketing, Engineering, Content, Sales

## Tagline
“Bringing elegance to aquatic life – globally.”

## Business Model Overview
AquaLuxe is a vertically integrated ornamental fish farming and aquatic lifestyle company serving local and international markets (B2B/B2C) with sustainable, scalable operations.

## Core Activities
1. Buying & Sourcing: local breeders/farms, international suppliers, plant growers, equipment manufacturers.
2. Selling & Distribution: wholesale (pet shops, zoos, hotels, distributors) and retail (walk-in, online, events).
3. Trading & Exchange: fish-for-fish trades, buy-back programs, auctions/bidding.

## Offerings
Livestock (freshwater, marine, inverts, rare/exotic), Aquatic Plants (incl. tissue culture), Equipment & Supplies (tanks, filtration, lighting, CO₂, decor, conditioners, feed and health).

## Professional Services
- Aquarium Design & Installation
- Maintenance Services
- Quarantine & Health Check
- Training & Consultancy
- Event Rentals

## Business Models in Use
Retail Sales, Wholesale Sales, Subscription Model, Franchise Model (future), Service Model, Export Model, Trade-in/Exchange.

## Target Markets
B2C hobbyists/collectors, B2B retailers/hospitality/designers, Educational, Export (EU/Asia/Middle East).

## Scalability & Growth
Digital expansion (e-commerce + online aquarium builder), export hubs, community engagement, franchise system.
