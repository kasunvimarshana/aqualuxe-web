Trade-In Program

Our trade-in program allows you to exchange your existing aquatic specimens or equipment for credit towards future purchases or receive cash. To begin, submit your items for evaluation, providing detailed information and images. Our team will assess the items and provide a trade-in value based on their condition, rarity, and market demand. Once approved, you can use the credit towards any purchase on our site or opt for a cash payout.
