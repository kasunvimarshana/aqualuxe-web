Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the care kits?

expand_more

Our care kits include essential supplies tailored to your chosen plan, such as water conditioners, plant nutrients, and maintenance tools. Each kit is designed to keep your aquatic environment healthy and vibrant.

How do I manage my subscription?

expand_more

You can easily manage your subscription through your account dashboard. There, you can upgrade, downgrade, pause, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes will take effect from the next billing cycle.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

expand_more

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades are applied immediately, while downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.
