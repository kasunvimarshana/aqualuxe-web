Global Fish & Plant Exports

AquaLuxe is a premier provider of ornamental fish, aquatic plants, and related equipment, serving a global clientele. Our export services are designed to meet the unique needs of international buyers, ensuring a seamless and compliant process from order to delivery.

Countries We Export To

We export to a wide range of countries worldwide, ensuring compliance with all local regulations and standards. Our team is experienced in navigating international trade requirements, providing a hassle-free export experience for our clients.

Licensing, Certification & Compliance

AquaLuxe operates with full transparency and compliance, holding all necessary licenses and certifications for the export of aquatic life and equipment. We work closely with regulatory bodies to ensure that all shipments meet international standards for health, safety, and sustainability. Our commitment to ethical and responsible practices is at the core of our export operations.
