Description

The Neon Tetra is a popular freshwater fish known for its vibrant blue and red stripes. It's a peaceful community fish that thrives in well-maintained aquariums with plenty of plants and hiding spots. Originating from South America, these fish are relatively easy to care for and add a splash of color to any tank.

Care

Neon Tetras prefer soft, slightly acidic water with a temperature range of 70-80°F. They are omnivores and should be fed a varied diet of high-quality flake food, supplemented with live or frozen foods like brine shrimp and daphnia. Regular water changes and a well-filtered tank are essential for their health.
