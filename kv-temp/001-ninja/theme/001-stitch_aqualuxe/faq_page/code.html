Care

How do I care for my new fish?

expand_more
Each fish comes with a detailed care guide, including information on tank parameters, diet, and compatibility. We also recommend a gradual acclimation process, instructions for which are included with your shipment.

What do I feed my aquatic plants?

expand_more
Our aquatic plants thrive with proper lighting, CO2 injection, and a nutrient-rich substrate or liquid fertilizers. Specific care instructions are provided on each plant's product page and in our online 'Learn' section.

How often should I clean my aquarium?

expand_more
Regular maintenance is key. We recommend a 20-25% water change weekly or bi-weekly, depending on your tank's bioload. Substrate vacuuming and filter cleaning should be done monthly. For a hassle-free experience, consider our professional maintenance services.

Purchasing

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for your convenience.

Can I cancel my order?

expand_more
Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement, provided they have not yet been prepared for shipping. Please contact customer service immediately to request a cancellation.

What is your return policy?

expand_more
Due to the nature of live animals, we cannot accept returns. However, we offer a "Live Arrival Guarantee." If your order does not arrive in perfect condition, please contact us within 2 hours of delivery with photographic evidence, and we will issue a credit or replacement. Equipment can be returned within 30 days if unused and in its original packaging.

Export/Import Processes

What are the import regulations for aquatic life?

expand_more
Import regulations vary significantly by country. It is the buyer's responsibility to be aware of and comply with their local laws. We provide necessary health certificates and documentation, but any required import permits must be secured by the customer.

Do you provide export documentation?

expand_more
Yes, we handle all necessary export documentation from our end, including health certificates and packing lists, to ensure compliance with international standards.

How do I handle customs clearance?

expand_more
Our shipping partner will typically act as the customs broker. However, you may be contacted by them or your local customs authority for additional information or to pay import duties and taxes. We recommend being available and responsive to ensure a quick clearance process.
