# User Guide

This guide is for site administrators and content managers who will be using the AquaLuxe theme.

## Managing Content

The theme comes with several custom content types to help you organize your site.

*   **Services:** Manage your company's services under the "Services" menu item.
*   **Events:** Add and manage events using "Events". If you have The Events Calendar plugin installed, this will be managed through their interface.
*   **Projects:** Showcase your R&D, sustainability, or other projects under the "Projects" menu.
*   **Partners:** List your franchise partners, affiliates, or other collaborators under "Partners".

## Theme Customizer

You can customize various aspects of the theme's appearance through the WordPress Customizer.

1.  Go to `Appearance > Customize`.
2.  Here you can change:
    *   **Site Identity:** Your site's logo, title, and tagline.
    *   **Colors:** The theme's color scheme.
    *   **Typography:** Font settings for headings and body text.
    *   **Layout:** General layout options.

## Page Templates

The theme includes several page templates for specific layouts:

*   **About Page:** A template for your "About Us" page.
*   **Services Page:** A template to display all your services.
*   **Contact Page:** A template for your contact page with a form and map.

To use a page template, select it from the "Template" dropdown in the "Page Attributes" box when editing a page.

## Shortcodes

The theme provides shortcodes for adding complex elements to your pages easily.

*   `[aqualuxe_subscription_pricing]`: Displays a pricing table for your subscription products. Add this to a page to create a pricing page.
