checklist Export Order Process

  1. Inquiry: Submit your export order inquiry through our website or contact our export sales team directly.
  2. Quotation: We will provide a detailed quotation, including product specifications, pricing, shipping costs, and estimated delivery times.
  3. Order Confirmation: Upon acceptance of the quotation, we will issue an order confirmation with all relevant details.
  4. Payment: Payment terms will be outlined in the quotation and order confirmation. We accept various payment methods for international transactions.
  5. Shipping and Handling: AquaLuxe partners with reputable international shipping carriers to ensure safe and timely delivery of your order. We handle all necessary export documentation and customs clearance procedures.
  6. Delivery: You will receive tracking information to monitor the progress of your shipment. Our team will coordinate with you to ensure a smooth delivery process.

local_shipping Shipping and Handling

AquaLuxe takes great care in packaging and handling our products to prevent damage during transit. We use specialized packaging materials and techniques to protect delicate items. Shipping costs vary depending on the destination, order size, and shipping method selected. We offer both standard and expedited shipping options to meet your needs.
