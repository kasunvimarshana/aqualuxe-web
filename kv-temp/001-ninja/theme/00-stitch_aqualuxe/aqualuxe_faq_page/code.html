Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

expand_more

Shipping times vary depending on your location. For domestic orders, it typically takes 3-5 business days. International shipping can take 7-21 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order has shipped.

What are the shipping costs?

expand_more

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the weight of your order and your location. We offer free standard shipping on domestic orders over $100.

Do you ship internationally?

expand_more

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees and import duties.

Care

How do I care for my aquatic plants?

expand_more

Each plant has specific care requirements. We provide detailed care instructions with every order. Generally, ensure proper lighting, CO2 levels, and nutrient supplementation.

What are the best practices for fish care?

expand_more

Acclimate new fish slowly, maintain stable water parameters, provide a balanced diet, and monitor for any signs of illness. Regular water changes are crucial for a healthy environment.

How often should I clean my aquarium?

expand_more

A partial water change of 20-30% weekly is recommended. The frequency of full cleaning depends on your tank size and bioload.

Purchasing

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Can I cancel my order?

expand_more

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Please contact our customer support with your order number to request a cancellation.

What is your return policy?

expand_more

We offer a 14-day return policy for unopened and unused products. Due to the nature of live products, we cannot accept returns on plants or fish. However, we guarantee live arrival.

Export/Import

What are the import regulations for aquatic life?

expand_more

Import regulations vary by country. It is your responsibility to check your local laws regarding the import of aquatic plants and animals.

How do I export aquatic plants?

expand_more

Exporting aquatic plants requires a phytosanitary certificate. We can assist with the necessary documentation for an additional fee.

What documentation is required for international shipping?

expand_more

A commercial invoice and packing list are required for all international shipments. Depending on the destination, other documents like a certificate of origin may be needed.
